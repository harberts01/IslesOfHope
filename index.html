Isles of Hope currently partners with 3 countries.

Zimbabwe

Isles of Hope International works with Tabeth Chicheta from Harare, Zimbabwe, who has a Christian ministry called Isles Zimbabwe. Tabeth has shared her testimony and a few of the women she has helped have also taken the time to speak about the work Tabeth has done. Please watch the video below and visit our Newsletters page to learn more.

Nepal

Isles of Hope International works with Sabina Chamling from Kathmandu, Nepal, who has a registered Christian ministry founded in 2012 called Rise and Shine Nepal. Visit our Newsletters page to read more about the work Sabina and Isles of Hope are doing.

Uganda

Our ministry in Uganda is a work in progress. We are getting affairs in order to help women in poverty, some of whom have been entangled in human trafficking. We know a group of native Ugandan women that currently work with these women, and hope to develop a partnership with them to lead ministry projects there in the future.
