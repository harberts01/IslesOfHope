Our Statement of Faith

Isles of Hope International is a Christian non-profit organization designed to help mostly poor women in countries around the world. The organization will provide support in the areas of:

  • Financial assistance (micro-loans) to start or expand a small business to help with the practical needs of women and their families
  • Spiritual teaching and training of all women we come in contact with

We will do our best to equip women to start small businesses to help financially supplement the ministries in the country, so as not to be entirely dependent on constant support from this organization.

Isles of Hope International will work to establish Elizabeth Houses, which are places where women can come to be ministered to, conduct Bible studies, and train for skills such as sewing or other business-related activities. These will also be places where micro-loans can be granted after an established business plan is submitted and reviewed. Church services can also be held here.

An Elizabeth House is a safe place for women to come to, open their hearts, and not be judged; a place where they will see and feel the love of Christ and be empowered to become disciples so they can go out and make positive changes in their communities and throughout the country. We will work with indigenous Christian women to administer and oversee the Elizabeth Houses.

These women are more knowledgeable of their country, culture, and language than foreigners are. We seek and look for women in these leadership roles who have biblical knowledge and counseling experience, as there are many women who come to the houses that have been traumatized by abuse, abandonment, and human trafficking. First and foremost, this is the Lord's ministry, not ours as a board. We only wish to do His will, following the commands of Scripture to help the needy and carry out the Great Commission.

The basis of our belief is in the Bible and God's infallible written Word. It was inspired by the Holy Spirit and written without error.

  • There is one true God existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. Each equally possesses all the attributes of Deity.
  • Jesus Christ is God, the Living Word who became flesh. Jesus Christ was fully God and fully man.
  • The Lord Jesus commanded all believers to proclaim the Gospel throughout the world and to disciple people in all nations. The fulfillment of the Great Commission is the main purpose for our lives and can be done in many ways, through the many gifts that God has given His people.

We believe all people are created by God and in the image of God, that all people need to be shown mercy and grace, and that all are in need of a Savior, who is Jesus Christ. It makes no difference whether man or woman, wealthy or impoverished, country of origin or residence, nationality, skin color, language spoken, quality of health, or physical/mental condition. Knowing of the love of God through His Son Jesus Christ must be told and shown to all.
