Isles of Hope currently partners with 3 countries.

Zimbabwe

Isles of Hope International works with Tabeth Kufamurase from Harare, Zimbabwe, who has a registered Christian ministry called Isles Zimbabwe. Tabeth has shared her testimony and a few of the women she has helped have also taken the time to speak about the work Tabeth has done. Please watch the video below to learn more.

Nepal

Isles of Hope International works with Sabina Chamling from Kathmandu, Nepal, who has a registered Christian ministry founded in 2012 called Rise and Shine Nepal. Continue reading for more information on what she is doing in Nepal to help single women, many of whom have children.

Uganda

Our ministry in Uganda is a work in progress. We are getting affairs in order to help women in poverty, some of whom have been entangled in human trafficking. We know a group of native Ugandan women that currently work with these women, and hope to develop a partnership with them to lead ministry projects there in the future.

1 Corinthians 10:33


Just as I try to please everyone in everything I do, not seeking my own advantage, but that of many, that they may be saved.

As a nonprofit Christian organization, we try to live out and do what the Bible says. We are sinners, certainly not perfect, and can make mistakes. We use 1Corinthians 10:33 as a guide for what we try to accomplish.
In the first part of the verse, where it says “I try to please everyone in everything I do”, we additionally look at Galatians 6:9-10 that says, “Let us not grow weary of doing good, for in due season we will reap, if we do not give up.” Therefore, as we have the opportunity, let us do good to everyone, especially those in the household of faith.
The middle part of the verse says “not seeking my own advantage”. As a nonprofit, we do not earn any proceeds for ourselves, put others first, and pray we will effect incomes for those in the world who are most in need. Our desire is that many of our brothers and sisters in Christ will be strengthened, spread the Gospel, and effect the lost to be saved. This is executing the Great Commission in Matthew 28:19-20.
By granting micro-loans, starting Elizabeth Houses, and more, our ultimate goal is to have those we minister to come to know Christ and advance His Kingdom, giving Him, the Triune God, all the praise and glory.
